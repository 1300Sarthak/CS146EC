Array operations: 
  Insert/update/delete
  Sort
  Search
  Element access is fast
  Can be static (fixed size) or dynamic (variable size)

Sets: 
  Insert/update/delete
  Search
  Sort
  Elements are unique
  Sorting may or may not make sense depending on situation

Hashmap: 
  Insert/update/delete
  Sort
  Search
  Most useful IRL
  Use key to point to value
  Can sort a hashmap technically
  Very fast element access and search

Others: stacks, queues, linked lists, trees, graphs
